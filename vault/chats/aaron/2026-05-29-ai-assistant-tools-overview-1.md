## 🦥 Slothitude — Product Description

**Slothitude** is an AI agent platform built around one core principle: true autonomy. Rather than relying on rigid scripts or constant human oversight, Slothitude empowers agents to reason, plan, and act independently — chaining tasks, making context-aware decisions, and iterating on their own until the job is done. Whether you need a research assistant that deep-dives across dozens of sources, a coding partner that writes and refactors full codebases, or a workflow orchestrator that ties complex multi-step processes together, Slothitude agents move with purpose so you don't have to. The platform is designed for multi-model support out of the box, letting you seamlessly swap between — or even combine — the best large language models available, from OpenAI and Anthropic to open-source alternatives, ensuring you always have the right model for the right task without vendor lock-in.

On the infrastructure side, Slothitude is fully self-hosted, giving teams complete control over their data, privacy, and deployment environment. Run it on your own servers, behind your own firewall, with your own API keys and model configurations — no cloud dependencies, no surprise billing, and no third-party ever touching your sensitive workloads. The lightweight architecture makes setup straightforward, while a clean developer interface lets you customize agent behaviors, tool integrations, and knowledge pipelines to fit your exact needs. Slothitude proves that powerful AI doesn't have to come at the cost of sovereignty: take it slow, stay in control, and let the agents do the heavy lifting.
